Highlights

Based on the analysis within this book - we can conclude that VER outperformed his teammate PER in Abu Dhabi 2022. Max goes on to win the race and Sergio fails to be beat Charles - therefore losing out on P2 in the championship. Fig. 1 and Fig. 2 highlight how VER was faster in Qualification - by both were he was carrying speed and how the engine was responding. Fig. 3and Fig. 4 in Race show how Max’s pace was more consistent despite being on a different pit strategy. Despite having the advantage of DRS, PER was unable to match Max or Charles’ pace throughout the race.
